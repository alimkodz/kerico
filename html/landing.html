Who we are

Kerico Healthcare Creating Value for our Community

Kerico Healthcare provides Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) services for organizations and thousands of rides annually. We are trusted by hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers to get their patients to and from their medical appointments with great customer experience.

The Kerico difference:

Our company was born from experiences taking care of our loved ones. We are committed to optimizing transport care with modern capabilities to create value for all key stakeholders, while keeping patients at the center of our approach.

What you need to drive for Kerico:

  1. Apply Online

    Enter your contact information into the form above and you’ll be on your way to becoming a Veyo Driver. In most cases, our onboarding process takes 3-7 days, and it only takes a few minutes to get started.

  2. Upload Documents

    Tell us what car you drive, upload your drivers license, insurance & registration, and we’ll start your background check. No need to find a fax machine, you can use the camera on your phone.

  3. Get Credentialed

    All Drivers need to complete a vehicle inspection, watch the training videos, complete a CPR Certification and provide payment information. We’ll walk you through each step.
